ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Sophia, and I am a recent graduate of Cornell University with a bachelors in Computer Science, and minors in Game Design and Behavioral Science. I am most passionate about creating projects that spark joy in people. Whether it be a fun video game where you shoot bubblegum, or a website that finds someone their perfect plant, I like to code things that add fulfillment to life :)

My most recent work experience was as a SWE intern at Atlassian during the summer of 2023. I worked on the company's Confluence External Collaboration Feature, that allowed paid users to add outside guests into their Confluence space. I was responsible for Guest Feature Awareness, a feature that was a part of onboarding Confluence Guests. I used React and TypeScript to code banners, onboarding modals, and feedback loops.

At Cornell, I was the Software Development lead for Engineers Without Borders, a project team that centers around creating solutions to problems in international communities. My software team was in collaboration with the local Tompkins Community Climate fund, coding an app that allows the community to offset their carbon emissions. The app was coded in React Native, and I was responsible for delegating tasks to each developer, leading team meetings, and coding the app.

In my spare time I enjoy cooking, hiking, skiing, music, and watching stand-up comedy.
