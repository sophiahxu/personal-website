Climate Fund App

React Native · Team Leadership · Organization

This app is a project I did with my project team, Engineers Without Borders. It was in collaboration with the Thompkins Finger Lakes Climate Fund. Using the app, users can offset their carbon emissions by donating a corresponding amount of money to the Climate Fund, which uses the money to support local green projects.

I was the Software Lead of the project, using Jira to delegate tasks and ensuring communication between the designers and programmers on the team. Within the code, I was responsible for ensuring the app could scale across all device sizes, as well as coding a large part of the users personal profile and history.

It is coming to the App Store soon!
