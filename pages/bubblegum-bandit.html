Bubblegum Bandit

Java · libgdx · Game Development

Embody a space pirate who wreaks havoc by flipping gravity and sticking enemies with bubblegum!

Bubblegum Bandit is an action platformer that I created alongside seven other team members over the span of three months. I was responsible for coding much of the physics and player mechanics of the game — most notably, all of the gum mechanics (how it shoots, sticks, and feels) were implemented by me.

It was coded in Java using the libgdx library, and is the project I am most proud of — not only because it's my most complex, but it's simply super fun to play!
